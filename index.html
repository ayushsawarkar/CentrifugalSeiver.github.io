Application In:

Centrifugal Sievers are used in controlled sieving of powder and granular products for classification, removal of lumps and foreign material.



The Centrifugal Siever has its proven application in the industries like Guar Gum Powder, Cellulose and Starch Derivatives. It is used to remove oversized material from grinded food products and cereals, chemicals, feed and minerals. They are sieved in Centrifugal Siever for uniform sieving at a constant rate.

Working Principle

The product is fed into the siever trough inlet with the feed screw providing uniform feeding into the sieving chamber, where the product is transported along the sieving surface by rotating paddles or brushes, which distribute the product on the screen. Feeding must be continuous and uniform for optimum performance. The finest material will exit via the central outlet connectable to any piping system. The material that does not pass through will be discharged through the side outlet.


Centrifugal Siever construction comprises a stationary horizontal cylindrical sieve. An internal rotating shaft fitted along the sieve length. In operation material to be sifted is feed into the siever inlet feed box. The Product is then uniformly feed into the stationary horizontal cylindrical sieve by means of the feeding hopper.
